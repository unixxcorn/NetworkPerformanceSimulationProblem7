When Server \(X\) is any server that has a mean processing time \(\beta\). We can detemine a processing time from the following function $$ F_X(x) = { 1\over\beta } e ^ {-{1\over\beta}x}$$ For Server A that has a mean processing time 10 seconds. The functions will be $$ F_A(x) = { 1\over10 } e ^ {-{1\over10}x}$$ and Server B that has a mean processing time 15 seconds. The functions will be $$ F_B(x) = { 1\over15 } e ^ {-{1\over15}x}$$ The question is to find the average of total processing time. Total processing time can be represented by \( F_T(x) \) $$ F_T(x) = (F_A * F_B)(x) $$ $$ F_T(x) = \int_0^x{F_A(x-\tau) . F_B(\tau)} d\tau$$ $$ F_T(x) = \int_0^x{{ 1\over10 } e ^ {{-{1\over10}}(x-\tau)}.{ 1\over15 } e ^ {{-{1\over15}}\tau} d\tau }$$ $$ F_T(x) = {1\over150} \int_0^x{ e ^ {{-{1\over10}}x+{1\over10}\tau}. e ^ {{-{1\over15}}\tau} d\tau }$$ $$ F_T(x) = {1\over150} \int_0^x{ e ^ {{-{1\over10}}x+{3\over30}\tau{-{2\over30}}\tau} d\tau }$$ $$ F_T(x) = {1\over150} \int_0^x{ e ^ {{-{1\over10}}x+{1\over30}\tau} d\tau}$$ $$ F_T(x) = {30\over150} ({ e ^ {{-{1\over10}}x+{1\over30}x}} - { e ^ {{-{1\over10}}x+{1\over30}0}})$$ $$ F_T(x) = {1\over5} ({ e ^ {{-{3\over30}}x+{1\over30}x}} - { e ^ {{-{3\over30}}x}})$$ $$ F_T(x) = {1\over5} ({ e ^ {{-{1\over15}}x}} - { e ^ {{-{1\over10}}x}})$$ and now we have PDF of interested model.
